Personal data (name, phone, order info) collected only for order processing. Not shared with any third party. Stored securely in browser LocalStorage. Only admin can access sensitive user data.

Users must provide accurate information. Confirmed orders may not be modified. Misuse may result in account suspension. Canteen authority may cancel orders due to unavailability.

Session cookies used for login persistence. Users may disable via browser settings.
